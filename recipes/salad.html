Salad Recipe

Description

A simple dish filled with healthy ingrediants and meats. Easy to make.

Ingrediants

  • 10oz Bacon
  • 3 large romaine hearts(lettuce)
  • 1 pint grape tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups Croutons

Cooking Procedures

  1. Make dressing: Add olive oil, lemon juice, dijon mustard and honey to jar. Add salt and pepper. Stir and store in cool place.
  2. Cook bacon at 400 degrees. Cook for about 13-18 minutes.
  3. Transfer bacon to plate. Chop into bits.
  4. Rinse the large romaine hearts and chop into pieces.
  5. Add all the ingrediants and stir. Pour the sauce over

Enjoy!
