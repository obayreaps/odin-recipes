Omelette Recipe

Description

An omelette is a dish made from beaten eggs that are cooked quickly in a frying pan with butter or oil. The eggs are typically folded around fillings such as cheese, vegetables, meats, or herbs.

Omelettes are known for their fluffy texture and versatility, making them a popular choice for breakfast or brunch. They can be simple with just cheese or more elaborate with multiple ingredients, tailored to individual tastes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Fresh herbs of your choice
  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Chop the herbs
  4. Put butter in the plan when hot
  5. Pour the eggs into pan
  6. Add the fillings
  7. Add your seasoning
  8. Fold the omelette

Enjoy!
