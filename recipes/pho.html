Pho Recipe

By Kevin Le

Description

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, typically beef or chicken. It is known for its fragrant, flavorful broth made by simmering bones, spices, and aromatics for several hours.

Pho is usually served with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime, and chili on the side, allowing for customization to individual tastes.

Ingredients
  • Beef bones
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Star anise
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Rice Noodles
  • Fresh herbs(thai basil and cilantro)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Hoisin sauce (option)
  • Sriracha sauce (option)
Cooking Procedures
  1. Prepare the broth
  2. Roast onion and ginger
  3. Simmr bones with spices
  4. Strain the broth
  5. Season the broth
  6. Cook rice noodles
  7. Prepare the meat
  8. Cook for couple hours
  9. Assemble the pho
  10. Serve with fresh herbs and sauces

Enjoy!
