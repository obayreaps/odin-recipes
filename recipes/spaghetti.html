Kevin's Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a popular Italian pasta dish made from long, thin noodles served with a variety of sauces. It is typically accompanied by a tomato-based marinara or meat sauce and can be garnished with Parmesan cheese, fresh herbs, and sometimes olive oil or garlic.

Spaghetti is a versatile and easy-to-prepare meal enjoyed worldwide.

Ingrediants
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Basic essentials: water, salt
  • Olive oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Italian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil(optional)
  • Pamesan cheese(optional)
Cooking Procedures
  1. Boil water
  2. Cook Spaghetti
  3. Drain spaghetti
  4. Saute onion and garlic
  5. Cook ground beeg or sausage
  6. Add tomato sauce and spices
  7. Simmer sauce
  8. Combine spaghetti and sauce
  9. Serve with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil

Enjoy!
